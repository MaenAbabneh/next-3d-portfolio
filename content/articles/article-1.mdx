## From 'Does it work?' to 'Does it feel right?'

Today, I don't just build websites that "work"—I build experiences that feel alive. As a developer, I specialize in bridging the gap between solid, high-performance systems and the magical world of **GSAP** and **3D** animations.

### The Tech Stack: Choosing Tools That Scale (and Dance)

I’m often asked why I don’t just stick to "standard" layouts. Performance shouldn't come at the cost of delight. My tech stack is a carefully curated toolbox:

- **Next.js & React:** My foundation for lightning-fast Server Actions and SEO.
- **GSAP (GreenSock):** Where the magic happens. I use it to make digital interactions feel natural and tactile.
- **Three.js & WebGL:** For when 2D isn't enough. I love pushing the boundaries of the browser with interactive 3D environments.
- **Tailwind CSS:** For clean, responsive, and maintainable styling.
